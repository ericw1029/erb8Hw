Customer Validation Rules:
  • Name: Required, 2-100 characters
  • Email: Required, valid format, unique
  • Phone: Optional, 7-15 digits with optional separators (space, -, +, (, ))
  • Address: Optional, any length
  • Error Handling: Rows with errors are skipped and logged
CSV Format Requirements:
Customer CSV Format:
name,email,phone,address
John Doe,john@example.com,123-456-7890,123 Main St
Jane Smith,jane@example.com,,456 Oak Ave
Bob Wilson,bob@example.com,+1 (555) 123-4567,789 Pine Rd
Example Valid Data:
  • John Doe,john@example.com,1234567890,Address
  • Jane Smith,jane@example.com,, (phone optional)
  • Bob,bob@example.com,+44 20 7946 0958,London
Example Invalid Data:
  • ,john@example.com,123456,Address (missing name)
  • John Doe,invalid-email,abc123,Address (invalid email)
  • John Doe,john@example.com,abc-def-ghij,Address (invalid phone)
